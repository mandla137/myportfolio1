Introduction

Once again, welcome to my portfolio. Take this journey with me as I will usher you in to the very core of who
Mandla Ndabambi really is.

Born and raised in Midrand, Gauteng, South Africa, I have been exposed to technology pretty much all my
life. This drove me towards having this sheer passion for technology in all its broad spectrum.

Having moved to Lesotho in the year 2004 I noticed at a very young that Lesotho is left behind in as far
as technology is involved. Upon completing my Form 5, I experimented with various technologies of
that time. This included exploring Microsoft DOS, Windows XP-2010, as well as becoming a
self-taught graphic designer.

Realizing that I can make a career out of my explorations, I then futhered my studies at The Vaal University
of Technology, where I became a certified Network administrator. I further pursued my Hon. in IT at LUCT,
as is on-going and have thus acquired web-develoment skills.
